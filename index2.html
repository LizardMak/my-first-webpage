My favorite Sandwich recipe

This is my favorite sandwich recipe

I am going to share this recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken cutlet
  • Bacon
  • Marinara
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Parsely

Cooking steps:

  1. Heat oven to 400
  2. Cook chicken for 4 minutes
  3. Pull out chicken and add marinara, bacon, and mozzarella
  4. Put chicken back in oven with bread for 2 minutes
  5. Pull chicken out and plate
  6. Sprinkle parmesan and parsely over sandwich
